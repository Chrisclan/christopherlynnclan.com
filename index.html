Who is Christopher Clan?

I am an aspiring Front-end Web Developer from Metro-Detroit, Michigan. I graduated from Colorado Technical University in August 2018 with a Bachelor’s of Science in Information Technology with a specialization in Software Systems Engineering. My GPA upon graduating was a 3.46. When I started school I wanted to be a software engineer. In my senior year I found out that Web Development was what I wanted to end up doing. I started saturating my life with all things Web Development. From listening to podcasts and YouTube videos on my 1 hr commute to and from my current place of employment to spending 3-4 hours a night on paid and free online tutorials. I haven’t ventured into much of the back-end development, but I eventually want to become a Full-Stack Web Developer.

From childhood to adulthood, I have always been infatuated with technology.

I fill up almost all my free time designing and creating things on and off of a computer. I absolutely love taking an idea from my brain and coming up with a finished product.

Learning is one of my most enjoyable activities. I love figuring out how things work and why they work. Not just with technology.
